The **Icons** tab is a searchable catalogue of single-colour, line-art glyphs. Because they are drawn in one colour, every icon takes on your theme's colour automatically, so a whole diagram stays visually consistent.

## Browsing by category

The catalogue is organised into categories so you can browse when you are not sure what you want: **Animated**, **Tech**, **People**, **Security**, **Files**, **Charts**, **Arrows**, **Furniture**, and **UI**. Open a category to see every glyph it holds.

## Searching and placing

When you know what you need, search by name (for example "server", "heart", or "calendar") and the catalogue filters to matching glyphs as you type. Click one to place it on the canvas, then resize and position it like any other element. Its colour follows your theme, so changing the theme recolours every placed icon at once.

## Line-art icons versus brand marks

These icons are deliberately single-colour and abstract. If you need full-colour brand marks, the official logos for cloud providers and infrastructure, look in the [Technology](/help/palette/technology/) tab instead, which keeps AWS, Azure, and similar icons in their official colours rather than your theme colour.

<Tip>
  Stuck on the right glyph? Search a synonym: many icons answer to more than one common name, so
  "delete" and "bin" may both turn up the same result.
</Tip>
