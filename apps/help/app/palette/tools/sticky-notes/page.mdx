A sticky note is a small coloured card for a short piece of text: an idea, a question, a label, a brainstorm item. It is the digital version of the paper sticky, and it behaves like any other canvas element once placed.

<Figure caption="A scatter of coloured sticky notes, the way a brainstorm wall looks on the canvas.">
  <StickyNotes />
</Figure>

## Adding one

<Step n={1}>Open the floating palette and switch to the **Tools** tab.</Step>
<Step n={2}>Click **Add sticky note**, then type your text.</Step>

## Working with them

Stickies move, resize, group, and recolour like everything else, and their colour follows the active [theme](/help/canvas/themes/). Scatter a wall of them for a brainstorm, then drag related ones together, or pull them into a [frame](/help/palette/tools/) or [group](/help/canvas/selecting-and-grouping/groups/) to organise the result.

<Tip>
  Sticky notes pair naturally with [dot voting](/help/collaboration/session-tools/voting/) in a live
  session, capture ideas on stickies, then vote on them together.
</Tip>
