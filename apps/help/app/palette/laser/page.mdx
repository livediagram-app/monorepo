The Laser Pointer mode gives you a temporary laser trail for drawing attention while presenting or walking a group through a diagram. Choose it from the mode picker at the top of the floating command palette, or press **`K`**.

## How it works

Move the cursor to leave a glowing trail that fades on its own. It is purely for emphasis: it draws nothing permanent and changes none of your elements, so you can gesture freely without altering the diagram.

## When it is unavailable

The Laser Pointer is disabled on an empty canvas. Add an element and the mode becomes available again.

<Tip>
  The Laser Pointer pairs well with live collaboration, letting everyone watching see exactly what
  you are pointing at.
