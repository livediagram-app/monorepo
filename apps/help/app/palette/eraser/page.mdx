The Eraser mode is the fastest way to remove elements from the canvas. Choose it from the mode picker at the top of the floating command palette, or press **`E`**.

## Erasing elements

- **Click** an element to delete it.
- **Drag** across several elements to remove them all in one sweep.

This is quicker than selecting elements and then deleting them, especially when you want to clear out a cluster of shapes or arrows at once.

## When it is unavailable

The Eraser is disabled when the canvas is empty, since there is nothing to erase. Add at least one element and the mode becomes available again.

<Note>
  Erasing removes elements straight away. If you take off something you meant to keep, undo the
  change to bring it back.
</Note>
