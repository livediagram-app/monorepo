When a diagram does not open, the cause is usually access-related rather than data loss. livediagram deliberately returns the same "not found" surface for a diagram you cannot reach as for one that truly does not exist, so a blank or "not found" screen does not mean your work is gone. Work through the checks below in order.

## Quick checks

1. **Reload the page.** A transient network blip is the single most common cause. A refresh re-runs the load from scratch.
2. **Check your connection.** The editor needs to reach the API to load a diagram. Confirm you are online and not behind a network that blocks the site.
3. **Open it from your own list.** If you own the diagram, open it from [your diagrams](/explorer/recent) rather than an old URL, to rule out a stale link.

## If you opened it from a share link

A share link is the most common reason a diagram refuses to load, and the fixes are different from a diagram you own.

1. **You may need a password.** If the diagram has a [share password](/help/privacy-and-security/share-link-security/), you will see a lock screen. Enter the password the owner gave you. A wrong password shows an error and lets you retry.
2. **The link may have expired.** Share links can be created with a lifetime. An expired link stops working everywhere and looks just like a revoked one. Ask the owner to **Extend** it or send a fresh link.
3. **The link may have been revoked.** If the owner stopped sharing or deleted that specific link, it no longer grants access. Ask for a new one.
4. **Check you copied the whole URL.** A truncated link, missing its share code, will not resolve.

## Still not loading

1. **Try a different browser or an incognito window** to rule out an extension or cached state. See [browser compatibility](/help/troubleshooting/browser-compatibility/).
2. **Try another device** on a different network to separate a local problem from a diagram problem.
3. **Confirm you are signed in as the right identity.** A guest diagram is tied to the browser that made it; a different browser or a signed-in account owns a different set of diagrams. See [working as a guest](/help/getting-started/guest-vs-account/).

If none of this helps, the diagram itself may be reachable but stuck on real-time connection; see [real-time collaboration problems](/help/troubleshooting/collaboration-issues/). If you are still stuck, [contact us](/help/contact/) with the URL you used and what you see.
